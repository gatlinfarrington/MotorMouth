Spell 2 Communicate (S2C) is an innovative text-to-speech solution that helps non-speaking individuals express themselves clearly and naturally.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the recommended age to begin S2C?

While S2C can be beneficial at various ages, age 5 is typically when literacy development begins. This natural learning window often provides an ideal starting point for introducing S2C techniques.

Is S2C exclusively for individuals with autism?

No, S2C is beneficial for anyone experiencing motor planning challenges (apraxia). While commonly used with autism, it's equally effective for individuals with various conditions that affect spoken communication.

Where are sessions conducted?

We believe in creating a comfortable learning environment for everyone. We'll work together to find a suitable location that ensures both convenience and optimal learning conditions for your specific needs.

What is the duration of each session?

Sessions are structured to last up to 45 minutes, carefully designed to maximize engagement while preventing fatigue. This duration has proven optimal for maintaining focus and achieving meaningful progress.

Where can I find more information about S2C?

You can find comprehensive information about S2C, including getting started guides and resources, on the I-ASC website.
